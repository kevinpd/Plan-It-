Young Students

TigerTiger

One of Manchester’s biggest party clubs. They offer cocktail master classes, karaoke and an extensive food menu. Eight different rooms, each with a different theme and ambience, there’s something for everyone at Tiger Tiger.

Treetop Adventure Golf

Treetop adventure Golf is the perfect place for students looking for something fun to do in the evenings, Located in the printworks Treetop adventure golf offers an 18 hole course where you and your friends can play while enjoying some twisted and tropical cocktails.

Mature Students

The Wash House

This cocktail bar is happily affordable, this bar is within the northern quarter in the printworks. This bar does require booking. There is no door charge. The washhouse has excellent reviews, as seen on websites such as tripadvisor.

The Lowry

There is always something new and refreshing to see as the artwork,sculptures and photography are changed often making every time you go a new experience. It also has a lovely cafe as well to sit and enjoy a cup of tea and cake as well as being close to the tram station making it an easy destination.

Best Couples

The Alchemist

In the heart of Spinningfields is the dark, theatrical Alchemist. The Alchemist offers a range of quirky and unique cocktails, served with a show worthy experience for those looking for a different way to dine and drink.

The Frog & Bucket Comedy Club

Manchester’s original alternative comedy club is the place to go after a long day in University, Monday night are especially for upcoming comedians building their craft as well as introducing you to brand new people. The Frog and Bucket has also welcomed huge names such as Jason Manford and Sarah Millican.
