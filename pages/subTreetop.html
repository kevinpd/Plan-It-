Treetop Adventure Golf

Treetop adventure Golf is the perfect place for students looking for something fun to do in the evenings, Located in the printworks Treetop adventure golf offers an 18 hole course where you and your friends can play while enjoying some twisted and tropical cocktails.

For the young students who love a great place to show off their skills at mini golf. It's great with a small group of friends all to get to know each other better. It has a great surrounding of nice places to get food and gives a real experience to the indoor mini golfing experience. Even for those 'pro' mini golfers this is a place to admire.
