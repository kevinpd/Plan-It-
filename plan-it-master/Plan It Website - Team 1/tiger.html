Tiger Tiger

Tiger Tiger is one of Manchester’s biggest party clubs. They offer cocktail master classes, karaoke and an extensive food menu. With eight different rooms, each with a different theme and ambience, there’s something for everyone at Tiger Tiger. They also offer free guest list before midnight every night, and offers on Thursdays, Fridays and Saturdays, including £2 shots and 50% of drinks.

This is a great place for young students who are wanting a great night out with friends. They are relatively cheap drinks for their atmosphere and the experience you will get from it. It is excellent on an all round basis for new young students especially in their first year as it is often a great place to find new friends and expand your social circle.
