The Wash House

This cocktail bar is happily affordable, this bar is within the Northern Quarter in the printworks. This bar does require booking. There is no door charge. The washhouse has excellent reviews, as seen on websites such as tripadvisor.

This is an excellent place for those Mature Students who want a great mid-day cocktail drink. With a wide varieity of wild and wacky drinks that they offer, it is a great place to spend quality time with friends. Despite its size you will feel most attached to this place and feel more comfortable than even in your own homes, with the great atmosphere and ambience that it gives off.
