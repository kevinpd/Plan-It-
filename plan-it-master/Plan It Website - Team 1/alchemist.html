Alchemist

Located in the heart of Spinningfields is the dark, theatrical Alchemist. The Alchemist offers a range of quirky and unique cocktails, served with a show worthy experience for people looking for a different way to dine and drink.

This wacky and wonderful place has a real modern and expensive feel to it. However the this place is reasonably affordable for those couples who are wanting to explore their tastes and find new great wacky cocktails. They have great vibes and the decoration fits their image to give you a sense of purity and great mood lighting.
